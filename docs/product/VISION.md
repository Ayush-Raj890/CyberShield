# CyberShield V3 Vision

## Mission
CyberShield helps people and teams make safer digital decisions by turning uncertain signals into actionable trust intelligence.

## Current Product
CyberShield is a multi-service platform with:
- AI-assisted scam triage
- Structured incident reporting
- Knowledge and awareness content
- Admin governance and moderation controls

## V3 Direction
V3 shifts CyberShield from a broad awareness app to a trust intelligence platform centered on TrustScan.

Key V3 outcomes:
- URL-first threat assessment workflow
- async scan lifecycle with progress and history
- explainable trust scoring and report artifacts
- stronger operational controls and legal-safe scanning boundaries

## Core User Types
- Individual users: check suspicious links before engaging
- Student and early-career users: learn safe decision patterns
- Security-conscious teams: centralize lightweight trust checks
- Moderators and admins: govern abuse, content, and scanner use

## Product Pillars
- Detection: fast risk scoring with transparent factors
- Intelligence: transform scan signals into explainable narratives
- Reporting: produce usable, shareable trust reports
- Governance: enforce safe-use policy and abuse prevention
- Reliability: stable workflows across frontend, backend, and worker services

## TrustScan Role
TrustScan is the V3 core engine. It handles URL normalization, passive checks, scoring, and report generation while maintaining legal and operational boundaries.

## Competitive Angle
CyberShield differentiates by combining:
- passive, policy-constrained trust analysis
- user-readable security explanations
- governance-ready documentation and controls
- end-to-end product flow from scan to report to follow-up

## Long-Term Direction
- V3: TrustScan foundation and scoring transparency
- V3.5: intelligence layer, trend views, watchlist workflows
- V4: scale via queued jobs, caching, tenant-ready governance, and SaaS operations
